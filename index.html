About me

Born and raised in Beirut, Lebanon. Immigrated to the U.S. with the hopes of being a living proof of the american dream. I am bilingual, fluent in both English and Arabic. Being from a country that has witnessed several cultures over history, I have a passion for traveling and exploring the corners of the world. My hobbies include but are not limited to: cooking, cycling, drawing, going to concerts, and meeting new people.


I hold a bachelor's degree in Genetics, Cell Biology and Development, attended medical school at the University of Minnesota for a little over 2 years, and I am currently a student at the University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp. I am currently working on expanding my knowledge and skills in the world of web developmet. After venturing into different career paths in the medical field, I realized that my passion lied within web development and coding. And finally, I'm a fun, ambitious, dedicated and driven individual who takes joy in problem solving and going out of comfort zones.
